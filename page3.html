Fact 1

Also known as Navagio Beach or Smuggler’s Cove, it is home to the rusty remains of MV Panayiotis, a vessel shrouded in mystery1

Fact 2

The beach is enclosed in a small cove and can only be reached by sea. The sea takes on an electric blue color due to sulfurous caves surrounding the beach

Fact 3

MV Panayiotis washed up on the cove’s shores in October 1980, making it one of Greece’s most photographed beaches and a sought-out tourist attraction
